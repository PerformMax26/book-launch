The Strategy

Campaign Phases

0

Setup & Hygiene

April 15 – 22

Non-negotiable pre-launch foundation. No ads go live until this is complete.

  • Finalize gateway landing page
  • Install Meta Pixel + Conversions API
  • Set up custom events
  • Build lookalike audiences (1%, 3%, 5%)
  • UTM structure finalized
  • Creative assets delivered
1

Spark

April 23 – May 7

Build buzz, drive to gateway landing page, capture emails.

  • Hero video (15-30s) — TJ speaking to camera
  • Carousel — "11 schools. 1 dream."
  • Static quote ads
  • A/B test gated vs open page
  • CTA: "Get early access"
2

Story

May 8 – 20

Deepen connection, share TJ's journey, build trust.

  • Long-form video (60-90s) — origin story
  • Testimonial clips from early readers
  • Behind-the-scenes content
  • Retarget all Phase 1 engagers
  • CTA: "Read a free chapter"
3

Launch

May 21 – 31

Drive purchases, event registrations, maximum urgency.

  • Countdown urgency ads
  • Social proof stack
  • Author thank-you video
  • Event promotion
  • CTA: "Order now"
4

Sustain

June 1+

Ongoing sales, retargeting, evergreen awareness.

  • Retarget all past engagers
  • Rotate fresh creatives monthly
  • Lookalike expansion from purchasers
  • Seasonal hooks (Mother's Day, gift guides)
  • Press/media mentions
